  # Privacy Policy

  Effective date: 05/10/2026

  ArtemisX built TikDrama as an ad-supported entertainment app. This Privacy Policy explains how TikDrama collects, uses, stores, and
  shares information when you use the app.

  ## Information We Collect

  TikDrama does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise
  location, or payment information.

  The app may process the following information:

  - App usage data, such as screens viewed, playback interactions, selected content, search queries, language selection, and app settings.
  - Watch history and favorite items, including drama title, poster URL, episode progress, and timestamps. This information is stored locally on your
  device.
  - Device and advertising-related information collected by advertising and analytics SDKs, such as device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, app
  interactions, crash or diagnostic information, and approximate location inferred from network data.
  - Network information needed to connect to online drama content, load images, stream videos, and display ads.

  ## How We Use Information

  We use information to:

  - Provide core app features, including browsing, searching, video playback, watch history, favorites, language selection, and playback preferences.
  - Load and display drama metadata, images, subtitles, and streaming content.
  - Show ads, including banner, interstitial, rewarded, native, splash, and app open ads.
  - Measure app performance, improve reliability, prevent abuse, and understand general app usage.
  - Maintain app functionality and improve user experience.

  ## Local Data Stored on Your Device

  TikDrama stores some data locally on your device, including:

  - Selected language.
  - Watch history.
  - Favorite dramas.
  - Last watched episode and related timestamps.

  This local data is used only to provide app features. You can remove it by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

  ## Advertising and Third-Party Services

  TikDrama uses Google AdMob and related advertising SDKs to show ads. These services may collect and process information such as advertising
  identifiers, IP address, device information, app activity, and ad interaction data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

  Google may use data collected from apps that use its advertising services. You can learn more here:

  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

  Your device may also provide controls to reset or limit use of the advertising ID through Android settings.

  ## Third-Party Content and APIs

  TikDrama connects to third-party content APIs and media servers to provide drama listings, search results, images, subtitles, and video streams. When
  the app requests this content, technical information such as IP address, device/network information, request headers, language preference, search
  query, or content ID may be processed by those third-party services as needed to deliver the content.

  ## Data Sharing

  We do not sell your personal information.

  Information may be shared with third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the app, including:

  - Advertising providers.
  - Analytics or tracking providers.
  - Content API and streaming service providers.
  - Infrastructure and technical service providers.

  These third parties process information according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

  ## Children’s Privacy

  TikDrama is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child
  has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

  ## Data Security

  We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is
  completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  ## Data Retention

  Local watch history, favorites, and language preferences remain on your device until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

  Information processed by third-party advertising, analytics, or content services is retained according to those providers’ own policies.

  ## Your Choices

  You can:

  - Clear app data or uninstall TikDrama to delete locally stored history, favorites, and preferences.
  - Use Android settings to reset or limit your advertising ID.
  - Disable personalized ads where supported by your device or Google account settings.

  ## Changes to This Privacy Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
